Why This Role Matters to Me

When I first read Trilegal's job description for Manager - Legal Technology, I felt a sense of recognition that's hard to describe. It wasn't just another job posting—it was a description of the exact intersection I've been navigating throughout my career.

The transformation I want to be part of: Legal service delivery is at a pivotal moment. I want to be at the center of that transformation—not just implementing technologies, but shaping how Trilegal envisions the future of legal services.
